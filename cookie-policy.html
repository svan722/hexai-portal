  1. What are Cookies?

    Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and track your activity on websites.

  2. What types of Cookies do we use?

    We use the following types of Cookies on the Website:

    • Analytics Cookies: These Cookies are used to track website usage and create statistics on how users interact with the Website. This information helps us improve the Website and tailor it to the needs of our users.

    You can choose to manage your Cookie preferences through your web browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to block or delete Cookies. However, blocking or deleting Cookies may limit your access to certain features of the Website.
